Tokens

The variables sheet — every type, spacing, radius and colour style, read live from @quire/tokens. This is the dial board: tune a token and every screen (and this sheet) moves with it. Flip dark in the bar to watch the semantic colours remap.

Colour — contrast & legibility
The guarantee. Every text-on-surface pair a real screen uses, checked against WCAG AA — normal text needs 4.5:1, large/heavy text 3:1. Flip dark in the bar to re-check both themes.
